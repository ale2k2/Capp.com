Intense storms, higher temperatures, increased drought, rising ocean levels, animal endangerment, and less food. These are all effects of global warming. Global warming, while it does have multiple causes, is dirrectly influenced by carbon emissions. As humans, this is one contribution to global warming that we can control. If we all play our part to lessen our carbon footprints, we can slow the progress of global warming.
