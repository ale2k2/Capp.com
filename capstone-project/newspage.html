What's Up With The Environment?

2023 is a year of confusion, fear, and concern for the environment, so it is important to stay up to date on what is happening around us. Here you can read some of the top stories concerning carbon emissions and environmental wellness.

G7 building consensus for faster end to carbon emissions

"Officials attending the closed-door talks indicated they expect a statement embracing a faster shift to renewable energy while slashing carbon emissions in the coming decade"

Airlines want you to buy carbon offsets. Experts say they're a 'scam.'

"Spending a few dollars to offset the footprint of your flight may sound too good to be true because it is, climate experts say." Is flying less the only real way to be more sustainable?

'Big Sponge:' New CO2 Tech Taps Oceans to Tackle Global Warming

Floating "mini-factory" on a boat pumps in seawater and uses electrochemical process to remove CO2.

China should help cover costs of global warming: John Kerry

U.S. climate envoy hails G-7 'step forward' despite lack of coal deadline. As the top annual carbon emitter, China should do its part to financially support poorer countries suffering from the impact of global warming, the U.S. climate chief suggested in an interview on Sunday.

Fatal bacterial infections could rise due to global warming, scientists say

The so-called flesh-eating bacteria can grow in warm shallow coastal waters. The infection can require limb amputations and about one in five people die, sometimes within a day or two.
